Where Primary Energy Goes

Each primary source splits into electricity generation and non-electricity primary energy. All values in terawatt-hours (TWh).

Electricity from nuclear, hydro, wind and solar is counted as the amount of fossil fuel it would replace, so it can be compared with coal, oil and gas (OWID's substitution method).
Source: Ritchie, H., Rosado, P., & Roser, M. (2023). Energy [Data set]. Our World in Data. Retrieved June 11, 2026, from https://github.com/owid/energy-data/ (based on Energy Institute, Statistical Review of World Energy; Ember).
